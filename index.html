Light as Ceremony

Creating spaces where transformation can happen.

For over a decade, I've been lighting Portuguese forests, outdoor gatherings, and ceremonial spaces. Not to impress - to transform. colored lights washing ancient cork oaks, colors pulsing through night air, fire and light in dialogue.

The honest answer to "why do you do this?" - I want to create safe spaces for psychedelic experiences. Environments where the container is held.
