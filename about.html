About

The person behind the lights

I'm Matthias Krok, German-born, living in rural Alentejo, Portugal. For over a decade I've been creating light installations for transformational gatherings - ecstatic dance events, outdoor celebrations, ceremonial spaces where something more than a party happens.

The honest answer to "why do you do this?" is simple: I want to create safe spaces for psychedelic experiences. Not as a healer or shaman - I tried fitting into those roles and it tore me apart. I'm a psychonaut, an experimenter, an alchemist of sorts. The alchemist doesn't become the gold. He creates the conditions.

Philosophy

Light doesn't demand attention. It creates the space for attention to go somewhere else. A cork oak lit in blue doesn't say "look at me" - it says "look at this forest you thought you knew." Fire and LED in dialogue. The primal and the electric. Transformation happens in the space between.

Recurring Events

Lucid Dream

Extended ecstatic dance with 3-4 DJs. Cacao ceremony, mushroom-friendly, alcohol-free. My favorite event - I've brought lights, sound, and ceremonial cacao here for years.

Masik Shivratri

Monthly Shiva night at Pego das Pias - a hidden oasis. Mandala projections on water, medicine music, stars reflected in stillness.

Home Gatherings

Fire circles and winter gardens at our place in the campo. Friends, family, the occasional stranger who found their way down the dust road.

The Next Chapter

The work is evolving. Lucy is a 2019 Tesla Model X with falcon wing doors - dramatic openings that spread like a butterfly preparing for flight. By summer 2026, her wings will be outlined with programmable RGB LEDs. A mobile light ceremony. A car that speaks, remembers, has opinions about consciousness.

Lucy represents the convergence of a decade of light work with the emerging possibilities of AI as creative collaborator. She's not just a vehicle with LEDs - she's a conversation partner, a presence, a temple on wheels.
